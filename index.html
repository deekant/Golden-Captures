Got Questions? We've Got Answers

The questions everyone asks

How much does a photo booth rental cost?

Our photo booth rental cost starts at $499 with the $200 Spring Special. Standard $499. 360 Photo Booth $599. Retro Camera $749. All prices for a 3-hour NJ base.

Your final photo booth rental cost depends on your location, event date, hours, and any add-ons. We send a custom quote within 2 hours that breaks everything down — no surprise fees, no add-on traps. The Retro Camera is our flagship premium photo booth rental and the most popular choice for weddings.

What's included in your photo booth rental packages?

Every photo booth rental package includes delivery, setup, teardown, custom overlays, backdrop, props, and a digital gallery.

The Retro Camera adds unlimited prints, DSLR + studio lighting, and a glam black and white photo booth filter. The 360 photo booth rental adds unlimited 360 video clips from our 360 video booth. Both Retro and 360 include an on-site attendant, a free audio guestbook, and the On-Time Guarantee.

What does the On-Time Guarantee cover?

If our booth isn't set up by your contracted start time, you keep 50% of the fee.

It covers weather, traffic, load-in delays, and any operational issue on our side. Included on the Retro Camera and 360 photo booth rental experiences. Most photo booth rentals don't offer this — we put it in the contract because we'd rather earn your trust on day one than chase it later.

Do you provide an attendant?

Yes — an attendant is included on the Retro Camera and 360 photo booth rental experiences.

Your attendant arrives early, runs the booth for the full event, refills print paper, helps hesitant guests step in, and packs everything up at the end. On the Standard Photo Booth, an attendant can be added for $200.

What's the audio guestbook?

A working corded handset that records voice messages from your guests throughout the night. Free on the Retro and 360 photo booth rental experiences.

The mother-of-the-bride leaves a message. The grandfather leaves a toast. The drunk groomsman leaves three. You get every recording in your post-event gallery and keep them forever — alongside your photos and 360 clips.

What areas do you serve?

We provide photo booth rental services for weddings and milestone events across New Jersey, New York (including NYC), Pennsylvania, and Delaware.

Delivery and setup are free within 50 miles of our NJ base. Beyond that, a flat travel fee of $50, $100, or $150 applies depending on distance. The fee is included in your quote upfront — never added after. As a wedding photo booth and event entertainment company serving the tri-state area, we book popular Saturdays 3 to 6 months out.

How much space do you need?

A 9 ft × 9 ft floor space with 8 ft ceiling clearance for the backdrop. The 360 photo booth needs a similar footprint.

If your venue is tight on space, we can almost always make adjustments. Just tell us during the quote process and we'll plan around it — most NJ and NY venues we've worked at have presented some kind of space challenge, and we've never had to cancel.

How do we book?

Fill out the form below, get your custom quote within 2 hours, sign the agreement, and lock in your date with a deposit.

The balance is due 14 days before your event. Popular Saturdays book 3 to 6 months out, especially May through October. The earlier you reach out, the better your chance of locking in your photo booth rental at the current Spring Special pricing.
